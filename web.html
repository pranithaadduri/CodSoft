Natural Language Generation

A Natural language generation (NLG) project involves the development and implementation of algorithms and models to generate human-like text or speech based on input data. NLG systems analyze and interpret structured data, such as numerical or categorical information, and transform it into coherent, contextually relevant narratives. These projects often leverage machine learning techniques, including deep learning and neural networks, to generate language that is fluent and contextually appropriate. NLG finds applications in various fields, such as automated report generation, chatbots, content creation, and personalized messaging, contributing to the automation of text-based communication tasks.
